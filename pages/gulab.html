Rose, belonging to the genus Rosa, is one of the most beloved and widely cultivated flowering plants globally. Known for its stunning and diverse array of colors, fragrances, and petal arrangements, the rose has earned a special place in gardens, celebrations, and cultural symbolism. Roses are deciduous or evergreen shrubs, and their blooms vary from single to fully double forms. With a rich history of cultivation spanning centuries, roses have been associated with love, romance, and beauty. Beyond their ornamental value, rose petals are used in perfumes, essential oils, and various cosmetic and culinary applications.
