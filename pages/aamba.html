Welcome to Shri Ganesh Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, PU. Ahilyadevi Adhyapak Mahavidyalaya Sangavi. Mango (Mangifera indica) is a tropical fruit known for its delicious taste and vibrant flavor. Believed to have originated in South Asia, mangoes are now cultivated in many tropical and subtropical regions around the world. The fruit is renowned for its juicy, sweet, and sometimes slightly tangy flesh, which comes in various colors and varieties. Mangoes are not only a popular snack but also versatile in culinary applications, used in salads, smoothies, desserts, and various dishes. Besides being rich in vitamins and minerals, mangoes are associated with numerous health benefits. The mango tree itself is an evergreen with a dense canopy and large, elliptical leaves, making it a valuable addition to tropical landscapes.
