Jujube or Bor, scientifically known as Ziziphus jujuba, is a small, sweet fruit native to Southern Asia. Also called "red date" or "Chinese date," it belongs to the buckthorn family. The fruit is consumed fresh, dried, or processed into various products like jams and teas. Known for its nutritional benefits, jujube is rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. In traditional medicine, it has been used to address digestive issues and promote overall well-being.
