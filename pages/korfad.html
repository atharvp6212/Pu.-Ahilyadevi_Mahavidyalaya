Aloe or Korfad is a succulent plant genus that includes various species known for their fleshy leaves and medicinal properties. One of the most well-known species is Aloe vera, prized for its gel-like substance found in the leaves, which is used for various skincare and health purposes. Aloe plants are easy to cultivate and are often grown as ornamental houseplants. Besides their popular use in skincare products, some species of Aloe have traditional medicinal uses, and they are recognized for their resilience and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
