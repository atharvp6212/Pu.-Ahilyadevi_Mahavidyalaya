Welcome to Shri Ganesh Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, PU. Ahilyadevi Adhyapak Mahavidyalaya Sangavi. Banyan trees are remarkable fig trees known for their expansive canopies and aerial prop roots. Native to tropical regions, these majestic trees have a unique growth habit where their roots descend from branches and take root in the ground. This process allows a single tree to cover a vast area, creating a natural, shaded space beneath its branches. Banyans hold cultural significance in various societies, often associated with spirituality and revered as symbols of longevity. These iconic trees contribute to the biodiversity of their ecosystems, providing habitat and sustenance for diverse flora and fauna.
