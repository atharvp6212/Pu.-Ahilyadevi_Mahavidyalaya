Bougainvillea or kagadi Fool is a vibrant and ornamental flowering plant known for its stunning display of colorful bracts. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, this tropical beauty belongs to the Nyctaginaceae family. Bougainvillea comes in various hues such as pink, purple, red, orange, and white, creating a visually striking appearance. Renowned for its resilience, it thrives in warm climates and is often used to enhance gardens, landscapes, and trellises. Bougainvillea's low maintenance and ability to flourish in diverse environments make it a popular choice for adding a burst of color to outdoor spaces.
