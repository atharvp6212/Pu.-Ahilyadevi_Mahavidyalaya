शेवगा

Welcome to Shri Ganesh Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, PU. Ahilyadevi Adhyapak Mahavidyalaya Sangavi. Shevaga, scientifically known as Moringa oleifera, is a versatile and nutrient-rich plant with a myriad of health benefits. Native to parts of Africa and Asia, Moringa is often referred to as the "drumstick tree" or "miracle tree" due to its various uses. The plant's leaves, pods, and seeds are edible and packed with essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. Moringa is known for its potential to combat malnutrition, promote digestion, and support overall well-being. Additionally, its seeds can be used to produce high-quality oil, and the plant is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine. The adaptability of Moringa to different climates makes it a valuable and sustainable resource for promoting nutrition and health.
